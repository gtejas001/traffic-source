AUDIENCE

The Audience module is where you define the segments of your users, customers, fans, and advocates. By clearly articulating the target audience you can more easily refine your project style and voice, and filter content based on segmentation and tagging. As your audience interacts with your project over time you can intelligently flag their behaviors or contributions to create dynamic communication opportunities.
